Emergency Services

Our emergency services are designed to provide immediate medical attention during critical situations. With a fleet of well-equipped ambulances and trained paramedics, we ensure quick response and quality care.

  • 24/7 ambulance service with GPS tracking
  • Trained paramedics and emergency medical technicians
  • Advanced life support equipment in all ambulances
  • Direct connection to nearby hospitals
  • SOS button for one-touch emergency alerts

Pharmacy Services

Our pharmacy services offer a convenient way to access quality medications and health products. With our online platform, you can order prescriptions, consult with pharmacists, and get medications delivered to your doorstep.

  • Wide range of prescription and OTC medications
  • Online prescription upload and verification
  • Home delivery of medications
  • Pharmacist consultations for medication guidance
  • Automatic refill reminders for regular medications

Doctor Consultations

  • Video consultations with specialists
  • In-person appointment booking
  • Access to medical records during consultations
  • Digital prescriptions and treatment plans
  • Follow-up consultations and care coordination

Lab Tests
